Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2025

At Lynn's Van, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

What We Collect

When you make a booking enquiry through our contact form, we collect the following personal information:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Preferred dates for your stay
  • Any additional message or request you include

How We Use Your Information

We only use your information to:

  • Respond to your enquiries
  • Arrange and confirm your booking
  • Follow up on any questions you may have

We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking tools, and we do not share your information with any third parties.

How We Keep Your Information Safe

All information submitted through our contact form is handled securely and is only accessible to Lynn, who manages bookings personally.
